May 24 - 26 2024


Welcome to the Wave of Change festival at Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk! For three days, we partner with Sea Shepherd to protect our marine ecosystems. From beach cleanups to live performances, every moment is a step towards a greener future—it's more than a festival, it's a movement. Join us!


Our Mission

At Wave of Change, we prioritize marine conservation due to ecosystem balance and threats like pollution, overfishing, and habitat destruction. Our mission: raise awareness, promote sustainability, and mobilize communities to act.


The Problem...

Our oceans are facing unprecedented threats from pollution, overfishing, and habitat destruction, jeopardizing the delicate balance of marine ecosystems and endangering countless species of marine life.

How You Can Help!

By joining us at Wave of Change, you can contribute to the solution by raising awareness, supporting marine conservation efforts, and advocating for sustainable practices. Together, we can make a meaningful impact in protecting our oceans and preserving their beauty and biodiversity for generations to come.
